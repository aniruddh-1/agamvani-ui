Privacy Policy

Agamvani Radio - Spiritual Music & Devotional Content

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: Agamvani Radio is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account with Agamvani Radio, we collect the following information:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, login, and communication
  • Name: Your full name for personalization
  • Profile Information: Optional demographic information (age, gender, location, language preferences)
  • Authentication Data: When you sign in using Google OAuth, we receive your email and basic profile information from Google

1.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about how you use our app:

  • Listening History: Tracks you've played, listening duration, and playback preferences
  • Favorites: Content you've marked as favorite
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Log Data: IP address, app version, crash reports, and performance data
  • App Interactions: Features used, buttons clicked, and navigation patterns

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT access your contacts or phone book
  • We do NOT access your camera or photo gallery
  • We do NOT track your precise location
  • We do NOT collect payment information (app is free)
  • We do NOT record or store audio from your microphone

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 To Provide Our Services

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Stream audio content to you
  • Remember your listening preferences and favorites
  • Provide personalized content recommendations
  • Maintain your listening history

2.2 To Improve Our Services

  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user behavior
  • Monitor app performance and stability

2.3 To Communicate With You

  • Send account-related notifications
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send important updates about the app (with your consent)
  • Notify you about new features or content

2.4 For Security and Legal Compliance

  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and safety

3. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our app:

  • Cloud Hosting: For storing app data and content
  • Authentication Services: Google OAuth for secure login
  • Analytics: To understand app usage and improve performance

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to us.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal requests from government authorities
  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Investigations of potential violations of our Terms of Service
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety

3.3 Business Transfers

If Agamvani Radio is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice in the app of any change in ownership or use of your personal information.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely on servers located in India. We use industry-standard PostgreSQL databases with encryption at rest and in transit.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Secure authentication with hashed passwords
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Regular backups of user data

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 90 days, except where we need to retain it for legal compliance or legitimate business purposes.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Update

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information stored in our systems
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format

You can access and update most of your information directly through the app settings.

5.2 Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by:

  • Using the "Delete Account" option in app settings, OR
  • Contacting us at the email address below

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including listening history and favorites, will be permanently deleted.

5.3 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of promotional emails by:

  • Clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any marketing email
  • Updating your notification preferences in app settings

Note: You cannot opt out of essential service-related communications (e.g., account security, Terms of Service updates).

5.4 Cookie Preferences

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect app functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

Agamvani Radio is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information from our systems.

7. Third-Party Services

7.1 Google OAuth

When you sign in using Google, we use Google's OAuth 2.0 service. Google's privacy policy applies to their handling of your data: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7.2 Links to Other Sites

Our app may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. International Users

Agamvani Radio is operated from India. If you are accessing the app from outside India, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in India where our servers are located and our central database is operated. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of information to India.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
